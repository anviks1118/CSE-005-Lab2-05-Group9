What is Spear Phishing?

Anvita Kisara

In terms of social engineering, one of the types of attacks that are included is called spear phishing. Spear phishing is quite like phishing; however, spear phishing targets a specific individual and send links, messages, or other ways to trick the owner into clicking them. From there on, it is easier for the hacker to steal or erase the owners information and/or data.

How Spear Phishing Works

Many people receive spear phishing emails which can regard in a payment decline, or their bank account being hacked. Normally the hackers goal is to make the owner clicks on the link which is provided in the email or message, and normally those links will “direct” them to a webpage. From there on, the hacker can easily access their data.
